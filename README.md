# PriceTale – Every Price Has a Story

PriceTale is a smart and simple web tool that allows users to track the price history of products listed on popular Indian e-commerce platforms like Amazon and Myntra. By pasting a product link, users can instantly see how its price has changed over time — helping them make informed buying decisions and spot genuine deals.

⚠️ Note: PriceTale is currently in development and not yet hosted.

✨ Features
🔗 Paste a product link to fetch and display price history

📉 Visualize price changes over time with interactive graphs

🇮🇳 Focused on Indian e-commerce platforms (Amazon.in, Myntra.com)

🧠 Smart backend scraper to collect and update price data

🕒 Helps users identify the best time to purchase a product
